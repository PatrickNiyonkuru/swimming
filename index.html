SWIMMING IS FREEDOM

The Pool Life

Swimming is more than just a sport for me. It is a way to clear my mind and stay healthy at the same time. There is nothing like the smell of chlorine in the morning or the feeling of cool water on a hot day.

Health Benefits

Every muscle in your body works when you swim. It helps your heart get stronger and it is very easy on your bones because you are floating. I try to go at least three times a week to stay in shape.

Mental Health

When I am underwater, everything is quiet. I cannot hear any noise or see my phone. It is the only place where I can truly focus on just my breathing and the movement of my arms.
